About the IMST

Discover the fascinating history behind the International Mathematics Symposium Tournament (IMST)!

The Founding of IMST

The International Mathematics Symposium Tournament (IMST) was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate mathematicians and educators who wanted to create a unique platform that combines humor, creativity, and mathematical problem-solving. Their vision was to break away from traditional math competitions and introduce an innovative tournament that would challenge participants while bringing joy and amusement to the world of mathematics.

The founders believed that mathematics, often seen as serious and intimidating, could be made more accessible and enjoyable through a lighthearted approach. Thus, the IMST was born as a troll math tournament, where the problems are deliberately designed to be impossible or absurd, providing a refreshing twist to conventional math challenges.

Evolution and Growth

Since its inception, the IMST has grown exponentially, attracting math enthusiasts from all corners of the globe. The tournament's unique blend of humor and mathematics has struck a chord with participants, creating a vibrant community that thrives on solving unconventional problems and sharing laughter.

What started as a small-scale event has now evolved into an annual extravaganza that captivates the imagination of mathematicians, educators, and problem solvers worldwide. The IMST continues to push the boundaries of what is possible in the realm of math competitions, celebrating the beauty of mathematics in its most playful form.

Join the IMST Community

The IMST welcomes all individuals, regardless of their mathematical background or expertise, to join in the fun and excitement. Whether you are a beginner exploring the world of math or a seasoned mathematician seeking a fresh challenge, the IMST offers a unique experience that is both intellectually stimulating and entertaining.

By participating in the IMST, you become part of a vibrant community that celebrates the joy of mathematics and embraces the power of humor. Engage with fellow participants, share your thoughts, and dive into the whimsical world of troll math problems that will leave you pondering, chuckling, and perhaps even scratching your head in delightful confusion.
